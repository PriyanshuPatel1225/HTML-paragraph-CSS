Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems. It includes learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding.

AI has transformed many industries including healthcare, finance, education, and transportation. Technologies such as machine learning, natural language processing, and computer vision are some branches of AI.

With advancements in AI, machines can now perform complex tasks, improve decision-making, and even interact naturally with humans, opening new opportunities for innovation and efficiency.
